About

About TravelRoam?

  • Whether you’re traveling for business, pleasure, or both, you can breathe a sigh of relief thanks to TravelRoam.
  • TravelRoam lets you roam to your heart’s content in 200+ countries worldwide without racking up expensive data costs.
  • Available on all eSIM compatible smartphones, TravelRoam saves you time, money, and hassle - there's no need to buy a local SIM when you arrive, which means you don’t need to worry about losing your main SIM.

Connect globally with TravelRoam

  • Simply choose your destination – either by country or by zone.
  • Choose your data bundle.
  • And you’re good to go.
  • Wherever you travel, TravelRoam will always find the fastest available data connection, so you can rest in the knowledge that your data will always be kept safe and secure.
  • So, before you embark on your next trip, get connected with TravelRoam.

Go where TravelRoam takes you.

We provide affordable, easy to use eSIM bundles in over 200+ countries worldwide. Less fuss, better deals on internet abroad.
